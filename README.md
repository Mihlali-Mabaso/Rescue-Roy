# 🦊 Rescue Roy

## Your guide. Your way out.

> *"When the ambulance is 40 minutes away, Rescue Roy is already there."*

---

## 📖 The Story

**It's 2 AM in Mangaung, Bloemfontein.**

A mother wakes up to the sound of her child choking. She has no internet. The ambulance is 40 minutes away. Her hands are shaking. She cannot remember the first aid steps she learned years ago.

**What does she do?**

Most people panic. Some call the wrong number. Others freeze.

But if she has **Rescue Roy** — she shakes her phone, says *"Rescue Roy"* in isiZulu, and the app guides her through step-by-step emergency care. In her language. Without internet. Without data costs.

---

## 🚨 The Problem

| Stat | Reality |
|------|---------|
| **40+ minutes** | Average ambulance response time in South African townships |
| **70%** | Emergencies that occur in homes (not public spaces) |
| **1 in 3** | South Africans have no first aid training |
| **R0** | Cost to access Rescue Roy's core emergency guidance |

**The gap between emergency and help is too wide. Rescue Roy closes it.**

---

## 🦊 The Solution

Rescue Roy is an **offline-first, voice-activated emergency guidance app** designed for South Africa's most vulnerable communities. It works on any smartphone, in any language, with or without internet.

### Core Features

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|---------------|
| **"I NEED HELP"** | One-tap emergency trigger (no confirmation, no delay) |
| **Voice activation** | Say "Rescue Roy" to trigger help — hands-free |
| **Shake to rescue** | Shake your phone to start emergency guidance |
| **Speech recognition** | On-device, offline: English, isiZulu, Sesotho |
| **Step-by-step guidance** | 10 emergencies × 5 steps each with audio + visual |
| **CPR metronome** | 100-120 BPM with voice counting |
| **Responder network** | Alerts 3 nearest community responders |
| **Offline mode** | All guidance, audio, and video stored locally |

## 🤖 uBuntu Assist — The AI Behind the Fox

uBuntu Assist is a **sovereign South African small language model (SLM)** built for emergency guidance.

### Today (Hackathon Prototype)
- ✅ Answers 20+ emergency questions (2 per emergency type)
- ✅ Works in isiZulu, Sesotho, English
- ✅ Offline — no data costs, no cloud
- ✅ Keyword matching + pre-loaded Q&A JSON

### Tomorrow (Vision)
- 🧠 Fine-tuned on 10,000+ South African emergency transcripts
- 🎓 Built by 100 UFS Computer Science students
- 🌍 Open-source, sovereign, offline-first
- 🗣️ Full conversational emergency assistant in all 11 official languages

> *"uBuntu Assist is not an API. It's not a foreign model. It's ours. Built by South Africans for South Africans."*
